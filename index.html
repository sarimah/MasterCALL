MPB 1343

Computer Assisted Language Learning

The course will introduce participants to Computer-Assisted Language Learning (CALL) topics such as definition, historical development, typology, phases, applications, theoretical perspectives and approaches, CALL software and website evaluation and CALL in research and teaching. Apart from reviewing research articles on CALL, classroom discussions and lectures, participants will get hands-on experience on how to integrate CALL in language teaching and research.
